Baldur's Gate 3 Gets Massive Update with Crossplay and New Classes

13 Apr, 2025
Photo by lalesh aldarwish via Pexels

Baldur's Gate 3 is getting a major update next week, and it's packed with exciting new features. Patch 8 is set to launch on April 15th, and it will bring changes like 12 new subclasses, crossplay, and a photo mode.

12 New Subclasses Added to the Game

The 12 new subclasses mean that each of the game's main classes — Bard, Barbarian, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue, Sorcerer, Warlock, and Wizard — will be getting an additional subclass. This is a big deal for fans of the game, as it adds even more depth and complexity to the gameplay.

Crossplay Feature Allows Players to Play with Friends Across Different Platforms

One of the most exciting features in Patch 8 is crossplay. With this feature, players will be able to play multiplayer with their friends no matter what platform they're on. This means that you'll be able to team up with your friends who are playing on a different console or PC.

Photo Mode Allows Players to Take Beautiful Screenshots

The game's photo mode will offer features like different lens settings, scene settings, and even stickers. This is perfect for players who want to capture the beauty of the game world and share it with their friends on social media.

This update is a big deal for fans of Baldur's Gate 3, and it shows that Larian Studios is committed to supporting the game long after its initial release. With new subclasses and crossplay, players will have even more ways to play and enjoy the game.

Get Ready to Dive Back into the World of Faerun

Patch 8 is set to be the game's last major patch, so you'll want to get back into the world of Faerun and experience all the new features for yourself. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a new player, this update has something for everyone.
