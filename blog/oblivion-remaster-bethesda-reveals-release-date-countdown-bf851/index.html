Oblivion Remaster: Bethesda Reveals Release Date Countdown

21 Apr, 2025
Photo by Kyle Loftus via Pexels

The gaming world has been buzzing with excitement over the past few days as Bethesda confirmed that a remaster of the beloved classic, Oblivion, is indeed on the way. After years of speculation and rumors, fans can finally get ready to dive back into the world of Cyrodiil.

Bethesda has been tight-lipped about the remaster until now, but it seems that they've finally decided to give fans a glimpse into what's in store. In a post on social media, the developer revealed an image featuring the roman number IV, which is a clear nod to the fact that Oblivion is the fourth installment in the Elder Scrolls series.

The announcement also included a date and time: April 22nd at 8AM PT. This means that fans will get to see more of what's in store for this remastered classic, including (hopefully) a release date. The news has sent shockwaves throughout the gaming community, with many fans eager to revisit the world of Oblivion.

For those who may be new to the series, Oblivion was first released back in 2006 and was met with widespread critical acclaim. It's considered one of the best games in the Elder Scrolls series, known for its engaging storyline, immersive gameplay, and stunning visuals. With a remaster on the way, it seems that Bethesda is looking to bring this classic back to life in all its glory.

A Perfectly Timed Release

It's worth noting that an Oblivion remaster is perfectly timed considering there hasn't been a new mainline entry in the Elder Scrolls series since Skyrim in 2011. While Elder Scrolls Online has seen numerous expansions, fans have been eagerly waiting for news on Elder Scrolls VI, which was first announced back in 2018.

The remaster is likely to be a welcome addition to the gaming community, especially with the current drought of new games in the series. It will undoubtedly give fans something to sink their teeth into while they wait for the next big release from Bethesda.

With the April 22nd livestream just around the corner, fans can't help but wonder what's in store. Will we see a release date? New gameplay mechanics? Or perhaps even some hints at what's to come with Elder Scrolls VI?

A Must-Play for Fans of the Series

One thing is certain: an Oblivion remaster will be a must-play for fans of the series. With its rich storyline, engaging gameplay, and stunning visuals, it's sure to transport players back into the world of Cyrodiil.

Bethesda has been known to create immersive worlds that draw players in, and it seems that this remaster is no exception. Fans can expect a faithful recreation of the original game, complete with all its beloved features and gameplay mechanics.

With the latest gaming news pointing towards an imminent reveal, fans are eagerly awaiting what's in store for Oblivion. Will you be tuning in to the livestream on April 22nd? Let us know in the comments!
