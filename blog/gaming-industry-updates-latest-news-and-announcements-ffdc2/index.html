Gaming Industry Updates: Latest News and Announcements

Published on 28 Feb, 2025
Photo by Garrett Morrow via Pexels

Apple has released a trailer for its upcoming anthology series based on the hit show Mythic Quest. The four-episode series will be available on Apple TV Plus on March 26th, coinciding with the finale of the current season.

We have to admit, we were excited to see this announcement, especially since we enjoyed the standalone episodes from the main series. Hopefully, the separate episodes will offer a fresh perspective and some new storylines that fans haven't seen before.

Super Formation Soccer Removed from Nintendo Switch

Super Formation Soccer, a game owned by Spike Chunsoft, has been removed from the Nintendo Switch eShop on March 28th. The game's removal is still unclear, and it's not known if it will be removed in all regions or just Japan.

This move comes as a surprise to fans, who have expressed disappointment over the game's sudden absence. It remains to be seen how this affects the game's popularity and whether it will be re-released in the future.

H&R Block Launches Roblox Experiences for Tax Season

As tax season is upon us, H&R Block has launched a series of interactive experiences within two other popular games on Roblox: Mega Mansion Tycoon and Club Roblox. The new content includes mini-games, puzzles, and even some tax-related challenges.

We're not sure how much the game's users will enjoy these experiences, but it's definitely an innovative way to educate people about taxes while having fun.

Ayaneo Confirms Flip Handheld Gaming PC Production

After initially confirming that its dual-screen handheld gaming PC, the Flip, had been discontinued without shipping all orders to backers, Ayaneo has clarified that the device is not cancelled and will instead see production of several hundred additional units.

The company also announced that it's committed to ongoing development of the FLIP product line, including a new Flip 2 model already in development. This is great news for fans who were excited about the original device.

CRKD Launches Gibson Les Paul Guitar Controllers

CRKD has just released a collection of guitar controllers compatible with various gaming platforms, including Xbox, Switch, PC, Android devices, and PlayStation.

The controllers feature Hall effect strum and whammy bars plus Bluetooth or 2.4GHz wireless connectivity. With these new additions to the gaming world, it's clear that innovation is happening in the industry, and we can't wait to see what's next.

Balatro's Poker-Inspired Solitaire Game Receives Update

LocalThunk has released an update for its game Balatro, which incorporates elements of solitaire. The developer acknowledges that poker is a significant influence on the game and states that it was inspired by this classic card game.

The company expresses confidence in their design decisions, stating that they've managed to bring together old and new elements to create something unique and enjoyable.
