Steam Deck Revolutionizes PC Gaming Handhelds

Valve's Steam Deck has been making waves in the gaming community since its release, offering a new level of portability and convenience for PC gamers. The device allows users to play their favorite games on-the-go, without the need for a separate console or computer.

The latest update from Valve promises even more features and functionality, including the ability to install SteamOS on other handhelds. This is set to launch in April, but the Preview branch has already introduced the feature for users to test and provide feedback.

Embracer Group's Rise and Fall: A Rare Glimpse into the Industry

The gaming industry is known for its behind-the-scenes politics and power struggles. One such story that highlights this is Embracer Group's failed attempt to buy up Lord of the Rings, which ultimately led to layoffs and restructuring within the company.

A rare interview with Saber Interactive CEO Matt Karch sheds light on the inner workings of the industry, providing a unique insight into the rise and fall of one of the biggest players in gaming.

Project Delta: A Potential Killzone Revival?

Sony Interactive Entertainment (PlayStation) and People Can Fly have announced that they are working together on a new game codenamed Project Delta. While details are scarce, fans of the Killzone series can't help but speculate about the potential for a revival.

God of War Celebrates 20th Anniversary with Special Content

Santa Monica Studio is marking the 20th anniversary of God of War with a range of special content, including Dark Odyssey-themed skins for God of War Ragnarök and a retrospective book set.

New Games on the Horizon: Upcoming Releases to Watch Out For

Ubisoft's upcoming game has generated buzz among gamers, thanks to its promise of customizable graphical settings and a locked framerate at launch. The game is set to release on March 20th.

The developer behind Vampire Survivors has created a website that offers a unique gaming experience, free from advertisements and clutter. The game itself is getting an Android port this summer.

Silent Hill F: A Spooky New Release in the Works

Konami has debuted the reveal trailer for Silent Hill F, which promises to be just as terrifying as its predecessors. While details are scarce, fans of the series can't wait to dive back into the world of Silent Hill.

PlayStation Portal Gets a Makeover with New Ad Campaign

Sony is giving its PlayStation Portal a fresh new look, complete with a busy dad-themed ad campaign aimed at families. The portal has been a staple for gamers since its launch nearly two years ago, and it's clear that Sony is committed to keeping it relevant.
