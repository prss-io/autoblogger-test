Gaming News Roundup: PS Plus, Kingdom Come, Avowed, Fortnite, Minecraft and More

Published on 10 Feb, 2025

Welcome back, gamers! Your favorite sarcastic commentator here, and I've got the scoop on all the latest gaming news, updates, and utter chaos in the world of gaming. So, grab a cup of coffee, sit back, and try to contain your excitement (or not, because let's be real, you're probably too hyped for this).

PS Plus Extra & Premium: The Good, the Bad, and the "Why Did I Pay $20?"

Sony's PlayStation Plus tiers have finally arrived, and boy, are they a doozy! For those who don't know, the difference between Extra and Premium is like night and day. Or rather, it's like trying to choose between a fancy watch and a slightly-less-fancy wristwatch. Both get you access to awesome games, but one has a slightly bigger selection of sandbox titles. Because, priorities!

Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 - Easter Eggs Galore (and Some Awkward Moments)

Henry's back in action, folks! The developer behind Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 has dropped some delightful Easter eggs into the game, and we're talking about the good stuff. I mean, who doesn't love a well-placed reference to a famous European fairy tale? It's like the developers are saying, "Hey, Henry, you know what would be awesome? If we included a nod to the Brothers Grimm in our game!"

Avowed - The Personal, Political, and Metaphysical

Avowed is an action RPG that's got everyone talking. And by talking, I mean it's got everyone wondering what on earth it's all about. The game seems to be a mashup of personal growth, politics, and metaphysics (yes, that's a thing). It's like someone took every philosophical debate, threw in some existential dread, and said, "Hey, let's make a game out of this!"

Fortnite - Fast & Furious Character Skins? More Like Fast & Frenetic!

Fortnite has done it again, folks! This time, they've teamed up with the Fast & Furious franchise to bring us some sweet character skins. Because what's Fortnite without a little bit of high-octane action and some seriously questionable fashion choices? I mean, who wouldn't want to rep Dominic Toretto's style in their pixelated world?

Minecraft - Dolphins: The Unsung Heroes

Minecraft fans, rejoice! The developers have shared a heartfelt update about the dolphins in your favorite voxel sandbox. It seems they've been working hard behind the scenes to ensure these underwater buddies are happy and healthy. Kudos, Minecraft devs! You're making us all proud!

Splatoon 3 - Best Chocolate Splatfest: A Tale of Two Teams

Splatoon 3 has closed its curtains on the second run of the Best Chocolate Splatfest, and it's been a wild ride. The winning team is... well, you'll just have to wait for the full reveal. But rest assured, it was a nail-biter until the very end. Or, you know, maybe they just ran out of chocolate.

Path of Exile 2 - Early Access Updates: Because You Asked for It

Grinding Gear Games has been busy updating Path of Exile 2 with new features and content. And by "new features," I mean they've added more reasons to justify the early access period. But hey, it's all part of the game, folks! After all, who doesn't love a good excuse to play for free?

Evee and Evolutions: Shiny Forms and Satisfying Evolution

Pokémon fans, rejoice! Eevee and its evolutions are getting some awesome shiny forms. Because what's more satisfying than seeing your favorite critters shine in the dark? It's like the developers are saying, "Hey, Eeveelutions, you know what would be awesome? If we gave you some sparkly skin!"

Ghost of Yotei - A New Protagonist, Because Why Not?

Sucker Punch has done it again, folks! They've introduced a brand-new protagonist in Ghost of Yotei, and I'm not sure what to make of it. Maybe they're trying to make up for the whole "last game was about some dude who got possessed" thing? Either way, it's exciting to see some new faces in the world of gaming!

Fallout - Prime Time for Live-Action Remastering?

Fallout is getting a live-action remaster, and I'm not sure what to expect. Will it be as awesome as the original? Only time will tell! But hey, who doesn't love a good dose of post-apocalyptic drama and some seriously questionable fashion choices?

That's all for now, folks! Stay tuned for more gaming shenanigans, updates, and utter chaos in the world of gaming. And remember, always keep your sense of humor intact... or not, because let's be real, you're probably too hyped for this anyway.
