Apple's Side Quest Series: Inside Mythic Quest's Forgotten World

Published on 24 Mar, 2025
Photo by FOX ^.ᆽ.^= ∫ via Pexels

The gaming world is always abuzz with excitement, and the latest news from Apple's upcoming spinoff series, Side Quest, is no exception.

This new series promises to take fans on an entirely different journey within the game itself. Unlike the classic Mythic Quest formula, which mostly takes place in the real world, Side Quest will transport viewers inside the fictional MMORPG.

A Glimpse into the Game's World

While some of the series' episodes focus on characters' lives outside the game, the final episode, titled "The Last Raid," offers a unique look at what the game looks like from within. Here, high schoolers Devon, Kelly, Rory, Seth, and Freddie embark on an epic quest to fight powerful bosses.

As seen in the exclusive clip from this episode, the kids have been playing Mythic Quest for years and have made an annual tradition of taking on big raids to commemorate their friendship within the game. This nostalgic aspect is sure to resonate with fans who have spent countless hours exploring virtual worlds.

Behind the Scenes of Mythic Quest

Interestingly, "The Last Raid" episode showcases the in-game world of Mythic Quest as a slightly dated World of Warcraft clone compared to its more recent cutscenes. However, this might offer a refreshing change for those interested in exploring what keeps players hooked on MMORPGs like Mythic Quest.

When Can We Expect More?

The entire season of Side Quest is set to hit Apple TV Plus on March 26th. Fans are undoubtedly counting down the days until they can dive into this fresh spin on the classic game and explore the depths of its immersive world.
