Gaming Update Central: Esports Shifts and More

12 Mar, 2025

Palworld's Crossplay Update

Palworld's crossplay update is coming soon, but we don't know exactly when it'll happen. The announcement states that it'll occur sometime late March, and fans are eagerly waiting for the confirmation. Expect to play with your friends on any platform within the next two weeks. For more: Gaming Industry Buzz: The Latest Developments

In celebration of its crossplay update, Palworld is offering 25 percent off on the PlayStation store and Steam. This is a great opportunity for gamers to get in on the action and play with their friends across different platforms.

Game Development News: Mike Verdu's Departure

Mike Verdu, Netflix's former VP of generative AI for games, has left the company and been replaced by Alain Tascan. This change is a significant development in the gaming industry, and we'll be keeping a close eye on it.

Royalties: A Look at Keita Takahashi's Situation

The New York Times reported in 2023 that Keita Takahashi receives no royalties from his quirky game series. While this might not come as a surprise, it highlights the challenges faced by game developers in terms of fair compensation.

Matt Mason's Latest Project

Matt Mason, the developer behind metafictional hits The Stanley Parable and The Beginner’s Guide, is returning with a new project. In celebration of MAR10 day, Nintendo dropped a new album on its Nintendo Music app - the soundtrack for Super Mario Bros. 3.

Gaming Hardware: AMD's Second-Gen 3D V-Cache Technology

AMD has delivered a major blow to Intel with its second-gen 3D V-Cache technology. This update brings several fixes and changes, including the Game Notes browser availability announced last week.

A nostalgic Question for Old Gamers

Do you have an OG Asus ROG All? This question might bring back some memories for old gamers who grew up playing PC games on this iconic laptop. If so, you might be interested to know that a new model has been released with improved specs and features.
