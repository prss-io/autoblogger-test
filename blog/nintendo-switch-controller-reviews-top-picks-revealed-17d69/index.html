Nintendo Switch Controller Reviews: Top Picks Revealed

16 Apr, 2025
Photo by Luis Quintero via Pexels

Nintendo has been making waves in the gaming industry with its latest console, the Switch 2. Recently, there have been several updates to the Switch's controllers, which are designed to improve the gaming experience.

The Best Nintendo Switch Controllers for TV Mode

One of the best Nintendo Switch controllers for TV mode is the official Nintendo Switch Pro Controller. It offers a high-quality, sturdy build that supports HD rumble, gyro motion, and NFC. The controller also has long battery life and is compatible with multiple platforms, including PC, Steam Deck, and mobile devices.

The Nintendo Switch Pro Controller is a top choice for gamers who want a premium gaming experience. Its build quality is impressive, with a satisfying bounce to the buttons and triggers. The directional pad is responsive and clicky enough to satisfy retro gamers.

The Best Switch Controller for Handheld Mode

For handheld mode, the Hori Split Pad Pro is a great option. It offers better ergonomics than the Joy-Cons and adds rear buttons for added functionality. The controller can also be used as a wired controller with an attachment set.

The Hori Split Pad Pro lacks motion, NFC, and rumble capabilities, but its comfortable design makes up for these limitations. Its size is clunky for travel, but its ergonomic design makes it a great choice for handheld gaming.

The Best Xbox-Style Controller for the Switch

For gamers who prefer an Xbox-style controller, the GuliKit KingKong 3 Pro and Max are great options. They offer deep customization options, Hall effect sticks and triggers, and support for HD rumble, NFC, and motion.

The GuliKit KingKong 3 Pro and Max also have wide platform compatibility and a built-in rechargeable battery. However, they lack a companion app for customizations and are pricier than some other options on the market.

In conclusion, there are many great Nintendo Switch controllers available, each with its own unique features and benefits. Whether you're looking for a premium gaming experience or a more affordable option, there's something for everyone in this list of top-rated Nintendo Switch controllers.
