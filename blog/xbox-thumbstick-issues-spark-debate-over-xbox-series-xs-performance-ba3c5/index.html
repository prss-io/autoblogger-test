Xbox Thumbstick Issues Spark Debate Over Xbox Series X/S Performance

Published on 06 Mar, 2025
Photo by JESHOOTS.com via Pexels

The latest news from the gaming world is filled with updates on the Xbox Series X/S, as well as new releases and special editions. In this commentary, we'll dive into some of the most interesting stories and share our expert analysis.

Xbox Thumbstick Issues: A Growing Concern

Gamers have been complaining about thumbstick issues with the Xbox Series X/S for weeks, but Microsoft hasn't confirmed whether these problems are related to a recent firmware update. According to some reports, the update added a limitation to the max range of thumbstick values and a virtual dead zone, which may be causing the issues.

As a gaming enthusiast, I can attest that this is a frustrating problem for many players. The Xbox Series X/S was touted as an improvement over previous consoles in terms of controller design and functionality, so it's disappointing to see these issues emerging.

New Releases and Special Editions

On the brighter side, new games are hitting shelves every week. One of the most exciting releases is Split Fiction, a co-op game from Hazelight Studios that requires teamwork to progress through its levels.

This game is a great example of how gaming can bring people together. The Friend Pass program allows owners of the game to invite friends to play without needing to purchase another copy, making it easier for gamers to find their Player 2.

Another highlight from this week's releases is the new special edition PS5 controller from Sony, featuring TLOU iconography and launching on April 10th. This is a great example of how gaming brands are using pop culture to drive sales and excitement around their products.

Election News Coverage

As we wait for season 2 of The Last of Us on HBO, Sony is putting out a new special edition PS5 controller that's sure to spark debate among gamers. This controller features a design inspired by the popular game series and launches just days before the show premieres.

This kind of marketing push is exactly what we need more of in gaming – innovative products that drive excitement and engagement among fans. By incorporating elements from beloved games, Sony is creating a sense of community around its brand that's hard to resist.

Media Analysis

The latest Xbox dashboard updates have raised some eyebrows among gamers. Some testers who installed the latest Alpha or Alpha Skip-Ahead builds reported that their consoles had been reset to default factory settings, with any games installed locally still intact.

This is a serious bug that could potentially cause issues for gamers, and it's surprising that it occurred in such early pre-release software. However, it's worth noting that early Alpha builds of the Xbox dashboard are often very stable and reliable, so this kind of issue is incredibly rare.

Incipio and Tumi Partner Up

In a related story, Incipio and Tumi have partnered up with a factory to produce protective cases for the Steam Deck. These cases are designed to be thin enough to fit inside the console's case, while still providing protection for the joysticks and screen.

This is a great example of how innovative companies like these can drive growth in the gaming industry. By creating products that are both functional and stylish, they're helping to build a community around their brand that's passionate about gaming.
